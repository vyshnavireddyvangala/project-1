Disaster Relief

Natural disasters leave thousands homeless and vulnerable. Mother Care Charity responds with immediate relief and long-term recovery plans to help communities rebuild. Disasters can be natural, human-made, or both.

Emergency Aid

We distribute food, water, medicine, and temporary shelter to families affected by floods, earthquakes, and other disasters. Man-made disasters include industrial accidents, fires, chemical leaks, and conflicts.

Community Recovery

Charities play a crucial role when disasters strike. Many organizations step in to provide immediate relief such as clean water, medical care, and shelter. After the emergency phase, they rebuild homes and schools, support health systems, and offer counseling for trauma.

Medical Support

Our teams provide first-aid, emergency medical care, and essential medicines during crises. Floods are the most frequent natural disaster, causing 40% of all disasters. Medical support helps prevent disease outbreaks and ensures people receive treatment during and after crises.

Psychological Counseling

We offer counseling services to trauma-affected individuals, helping them cope with stress, fear, and emotional loss. Psychological support helps survivors deal with grief and trauma so they can recover emotionally and regain their sense of safety.
